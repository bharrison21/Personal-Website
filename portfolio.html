Projects

Fyuzed

Lanching this summer, my company, Fyuzed LLC, was the solution to college students crippling stress: a platform that recommends to them what classes to take, clubs to join, and jobs to recruit for. We utilize AI recommendation algorithms, a Django backend, and reactJS frontend, to make this the most user-friendly and effective platform as possible.

Study Buddies

In December of 2020, I joined the Study Buddies team to help them grow and advance their product. Study Buddies is a simple, yet neat, idea - a program that creates study groups for like-minded students who would struggle to meet each other during online school. To date, Study Buddies has helped over 18,000 students find friends to study with at 3 universities. Learn more about study buddies here!

Smart Mirror

Over quarantine, I built smart mirrors through a rasberry pi connecting to a monitor and a semi-transparent mirror. I manipulated the GUI programs that were already created to customize each screen to the users' expectation. This project allowed me to learn more about editting vast JSON files and communicating clearly with clients about what they wanted to ensure they were satisfied.
